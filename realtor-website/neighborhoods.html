San Francisco

Pacific Heights

Perched above the city, Pacific Heights is San Francisco's most prestigious neighborhood, renowned for its stunning bay views and impressive Victorian and Edwardian mansions. Home to tech titans, established families, and business leaders, this exclusive enclave offers tree-lined streets and world-class dining.

Median Price: $5.2M Walk Score: 94 Notable: Historic Mansions

San Francisco

Noe Valley

Sunny, safe, and sophisticated, Noe Valley is the quintessential San Francisco neighborhood for families and young professionals. Centered around vibrant 24th Street with its boutiques, cafes, and restaurants, this charming enclave boasts excellent schools and a strong sense of community.

Median Price: $2.2M Walk Score: 92 Notable: Top Schools

Palo Alto

Synonymous with innovation and excellence, Palo Alto is the heart of Silicon Valley and home to Stanford University. This prestigious community combines cutting-edge technology culture with tree-lined streets, excellent schools, and sophisticated dining and shopping on University Avenue.

Median Price: $3.5M Walk Score: 80 Notable: Tech Hub

Mill Valley

Nestled in the Marin hills and surrounded by magnificent redwood forests, Mill Valley offers the perfect blend of suburban tranquility and outdoor adventure. This artistic, progressive community is beloved by nature enthusiasts, families, and those seeking a slower pace without sacrificing sophistication.

Median Price: $2.0M Walk Score: 68 Notable: Outdoor Paradise

East Bay

Berkeley

Vibrant, progressive, and culturally rich, Berkeley is home to UC Berkeley and a thriving community of artists, academics, and entrepreneurs. With world-class restaurants, eclectic shopping, stunning views of the Bay, and a strong commitment to sustainability, Berkeley appeals to those seeking intellectual stimulation and authentic culture.

Median Price: $1.5M Walk Score: 88 Notable: Arts & Dining

Marin County

Sausalito

Perched on the Marin waterfront with iconic views of the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco skyline, Sausalito is an enchanting village of galleries, studios, and waterfront restaurants. This bohemian community attracts artists, creatives, and those who value beauty, community, and a laid-back coastal lifestyle.

Median Price: $1.8M Walk Score: 72 Notable: Bay Views
